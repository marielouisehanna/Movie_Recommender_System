How It Works

User-Based Collaborative Filtering

Recommends movies based on similar users' preferences. People who liked the same movies as you also enjoyed these.

Item-Based Collaborative Filtering

Recommends movies similar to ones you've already rated highly. If you liked this movie, you might also enjoy these.

Global Baseline Estimate

Uses global trends, user biases, and item popularity to make personalized predictions about movies you might enjoy.
